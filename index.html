Privacy Policy

Last updated: 28.12.2025

Balance Drop (“the App”) is a minimalist arcade game. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected and used when you use the App.

Information Collection and Use

Balance Drop itself does not collect or store any personal information.

The App displays advertisements provided by third-party advertising services. These services may automatically collect certain information in order to deliver ads.

This information may include:

  • device identifiers (such as Advertising ID)
  • approximate location (country or region)
  • ad interaction and usage data

This data is collected directly by advertising providers, not by the developer.

Third-Party Services

The App uses third-party advertising services that may collect information used to identify you.

Examples:

  • Google AdMob – Privacy Policy

Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. Advertising providers may collect limited data as described above.

Data Security

The App does not store or transmit personal data. All advertising-related data handling is performed by third-party providers.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated. Any changes will be posted on this page.
